Steak Sandwich

Description

Are you ready for this mouth watering and easy to make sandwich?

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 pound thinly sliced sirloin steak strips
  • 8 ounces sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and cut into strips
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 10 slices provolone cheese
  • 1 loaf French bread
  • 1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup Pinot Noir or other dry red wine

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the beef, and cook until browned. Add the mushrooms, bell pepper and onion;cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Combine the beef broth, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, red pepper falkes and red wine.
  3. Transfer the beef and vegetables to the slow cooker, stirring to blend. Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours until beef is tender.
  4. Preheat oven to 425 degree Fahrenheit (220 degrees Celsius).
  5. Drain liquid from slow cooker and save for use as a dipping sauce.
  6. Slice French bread loaf lengthwise like a submarine sandwich.
  7. Mix together the horseradish and mustard in a small bowl;spread sauce onto the inside of the loaf.
  8. Place slices of provolone cheese on both on both sides of the loaf, then fill with beef and vegetables. Close the loaf and wrap the entire sandwich with aluminum foil.
  9. Bake for 10-15 minutes in the oven on middle rack.
  10. Slice into servings, and serve with the juices from the slow cooker for use as a dipping sauce.
