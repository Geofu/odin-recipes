Spaghetti

Description

This is an all-time classic recipe! We will be showing you how to make this delicious dish in absolutely no time at all!

Ingredients

  • 2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 (150 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teapsoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon died basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 ounces of uncooked noodles
  • 8 ounces of water

Steps

  1. Combine diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, garlic powder, sugar, parsley, salt, oregano, basil, and pepper in a saucepan.
  2. Bring sauce to a boil.
  3. Lower heat to medium-low, cover saucepan, and simmer for about 30 minutes.
  4. In a separate pot, fill with 8 ounces of water and bring to boil.
  5. Place noodles in pot. Cook noodles for about 9-10 minutes until soft, stirring occassionally.
  6. Strain cooked noodles in a large strainer.
  7. Place cooked noodles into sauce pan and stir until sauce thoroughly covers noodles.
